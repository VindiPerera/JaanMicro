About Arrears

Arrears: The total amount that remains to be paid by customers.

Principal Outstanding: Original loan/pawning amount not yet repaid.

Interest Outstanding:

  • Flat rate loans (Type 1, Type 4 Micro, 54 Daily): Total fixed interest minus interest paid
  • Reducing balance loans: Accrued interest plus estimated future interest on remaining principal

Penalties: Additional charges for late payments.

Overdue: Accounts past their maturity date.
