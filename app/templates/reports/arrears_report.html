About Arrears

Past Maturity: Loans/pawnings that have passed their maturity date with outstanding balance.

Installment Overdue: Active loans (before maturity) that have overdue installments — fully unpaid or partially paid.

Partial Overdue: Installments that were partially paid, with remaining balance counted as overdue.

Advance Balance: Overpayment credit — excess amount from previous payments auto-deducted in next payment.

This report includes both past maturity accounts and active loans with overdue installments.
